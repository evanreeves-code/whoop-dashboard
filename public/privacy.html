Privacy Policy

Morning Dashboard — Last updated February 2026

Overview

Morning Dashboard is a personal app that connects to your Whoop account to display your health and recovery data. This policy explains what data is accessed and how it is used.

Data Collected

With your explicit authorization via Whoop's OAuth flow, this app accesses:

  • Recovery score, HRV, and resting heart rate
  • Sleep performance and sleep data
  • Workout and strain data

How Data Is Used

Your Whoop data is used solely to display your personal morning dashboard. It is not sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party. All data is stored locally on your own device.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored in a local SQLite database on your machine. No data is sent to external servers beyond the Whoop API itself.

Contact

This is a personal-use application. For questions, contact the app owner directly.
